About the Program

A Student-Led Movement, Built for Young Voices

The Summer Intensive Speech & Debate program is a 100% volunteer initiative conceived and led by student mentors Sriram Surapaneni and Maira Lala.

Their mission is simple: give every young person the tools to think critically, speak confidently, and argue persuasively — no matter where they live.

No fees. No barriers. Just rigorous, joyful learning delivered live via Zoom from mentors who've been in the room and want to bring others with them.

The Curriculum

What Students Will Learn

Our intensive curriculum builds real-world skills across three core pillars — from crafting a compelling argument to holding your own on a competitive stage.

Persuasive Speaking

Students learn the art of rhetoric — how to structure a speech, command an audience, and deliver ideas with clarity and confidence.

Research Skills

From finding credible sources to synthesizing complex information, students develop the critical thinking foundation that powers every great argument.

Competitive Debate

Students engage in structured debate formats, learning to listen actively, rebut effectively, and think on their feet under real pressure.
